Core Services

Four Pillars of African Advisory

Each service is designed to remove a specific barrier between your organisation and successful market outcomes in Africa.

01

Market Intelligence

We deliver bespoke, evidence-based intelligence that gives your organisation a clear-eyed view of the African markets you're entering or operating in. We track and analyse political shifts, regulatory changes, market trajectories, competitive dynamics, and commercial trends so your leadership team always has the intelligence advantage. Our business intelligence reports cover Total Addressable Market (TAM), target audience mapping, customer segmentation, channel analysis, and pipeline development — turning raw market data into strategic decisions.

TAM Analysis Audience Mapping Competitive Tracking Pipeline Development Channel Analysis

02

Country Assessment

Before a single dollar is committed, you need to understand the full political-economic landscape of your target market. Our country assessments give you a comprehensive, board-ready evaluation of the risks, opportunities, and strategic pathways available in any African jurisdiction. We assess the political economy, extractives environment, regulatory frameworks, and stakeholder landscape. We build entry and exit strategies and lead scenario planning exercises tailored to your risk appetite and commercial ambitions.

Risk Mapping Entry Strategy Scenario Planning Opportunity ID Board-Level Reporting

03

Stakeholder Relationship Management

In Africa, relationships are infrastructure. The most technically sound project can stall without the right government relationships, community support, or industry alliances. We build and manage the stakeholder ecosystems that keep your projects moving. We provide stakeholder profiling of key decision-makers, design engagement strategies, and offer hands-on government relations support. Our approach sits at the intersection of politics, regulation, and commerce — always aligned with your commercial objectives.

Government Relations Stakeholder Profiling Issues Advocacy Engagement Strategy

04

Permitting & Compliance

Africa's regulatory environments are complex, layered, and fast-moving. Navigating them requires more than legal knowledge — it requires relationships, timing, and strategic intelligence. We serve as your outsourced compliance function, managing the full lifecycle of regulatory engagement on your behalf. We deliver permitting blueprints and timelines, execute active tenement management, manage licenses and renewals, and provide strategic advisory on local content regulations.

Tenement Management Permits & Licenses Local Content Regulatory Mapping Compliance
